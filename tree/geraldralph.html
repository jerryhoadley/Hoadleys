82 Gerald Ralph ("Jerry")

(William White #65, James Francis III #51, James Francis, Jr. #43, James Francis #8, Elias #2, Thomas #1), born 23 July, 1954 in Escondido, Calif.

Born 23 July, 1954 in Escondido, California. Married 13 September, 1980 in Escondido, CA, to Ramona (Mona) Kay Trouten, daughter of Robert. and Jessie Trouten, born 14 August, 1915 in Riverside.

When Jerry Hoadley was born on 23 July, 1954, in Escondido, California. His father, William White Hoadley, was 40 and his mother, Dorothy May Johnson, was 40. He was born in and grew up in Escondido, California, and attended Escondido High School. Jerry ran Cross Country and the Track middle dinstances and played Basketball. Jerry was awarded "Most Valuable" in Track his senior year and received eight Varsity letters. After High School, Jerry attended Palomar Junior College in San Marcos for a year and then joined the Air Force. In the Air Force, Jerry was trained and an Avionice Systems Specialist and spent his time as a member of the F-15 Joint Test Force at Edwards Air Force Base in California. After his time in the Air Froce, Jerry Graduated from San Tiego State University with a Bachelors degree in Business Administration with enphasis on Information Systems (computor engineering).

After College, Jerry worked 40m years for the NCR/Teradata organizations as an engineer and as a manager. During his career, Jerry went from key punching his Cobol code on a Key Punch machine and feeding the cards into a card reader to Managing a large international team suporting a databases capable of quickly processing pedabytes of information for quick answers. Jerry fully appreciates how quickly this will seem elementary as we approach AI solutions and to concept of a Data Base fades.

Jerry retired in 2020 and and he and Mona spend time in both California and Tennessee.
