The Hoadley Family

James Francis Hoadley III

JAMES FRANCIS III born 10 April, 1884 in Cloverdale, calif.; died 13 December, 1954 in Perris, Calif. Married 16 October, 1912 in Hollister, Calif. to DOROTHY BURNTSIDE WHITE, daughter of Enoch A. and Arletta (Van Dutter) White, born 9 April, 1890 at Burntside Lake, Minnesota, died 22 September, 1965 in Long Beach, Calif. Both are buried in Escondido, Calif.

James Francis, born in Utah, attended school in Redding, Calif. He graduated from Chico High School in 1904 and from State College in 1906. He taught school in Amador City, Calif., and served as the Principal in Redding, Calif. He was also the Secretary of Elk's Club and studied law. He later served as the Principal in San Ramon and as Vice President in Crockett, Calif. He held the position of Principal in Perris, Calif., and was a member of the Co. Board of Education. He held various offices in local and District Lion's Clubs and pursued further studies at the University of California, Redlands Unit, and San Diego State.

James, known as Frank to his friends and relatives, lost an arm in a hunting accident, at age 14, but this did not deter him in his ambition to become an Educator. He started his teaching career in the Crockett-Martinez area, and over the years gradually worked his way south to the southern portion of the State, following his profession, namely: San Ramon, Hollister, Morgan Hill and Perris, Calif. He ended his career before his retirement, as Superintendent of Schools.

Dorothy Burntside White (Gramie) was born in Burntside Lake, Minnesota (registered in Ely, April 1890). She lived in Marion, Iowa, from 1893 to 1896, and in Ely, Minnesota, from 1896 to 1899. She resided in Baker, Oregon, from 1899 to 1900 and returned to Ely, Minnesota, in 1905. In 1906, she moved to Baker, Oregon, and later to Bristol, Michigan, in 1908. From 1908 to 1909, she lived in Berkeley, California.

  2. (65) William White, born 24 Aug. 1915. Married Dorothy Ethel Johnson
  3. (66) Philip Miner, born 2 July, 1918 in Morgan Hill, CA,(to be with family doctor who had moved). Married Lois Eva Blake, 20 Sept 1942 of San Banadino, CA.
