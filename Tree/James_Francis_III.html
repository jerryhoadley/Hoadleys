The Hoadley Family

James Francis Hoadley III

JAMES FRANCIS III born 10 April, 1884 in Cloverdale, calif.; died 13 December, 1954 in Perris, Calif. Married 16 October, 1912 in Hollister, Calif. to DOROTHY BURNTSIDE WHITE, daughter of Enoch A. and Arletta (Van Dutter) White, born 9 April, 1890 at Burntside Lake, Minnesota, died 22 September, 1965 in Long Beach, Calif. Both are buried in Escondido, Calif.

James Francis Hoadley, known as Frank to his friends and relatives, lost an arm in a hunting accident, at age 14, but this did not deter him in his ambition to become an Educator. He started his teaching career in the Crockett-Martinez area, and over the years gradually worked his way south to the southern portion of the State, following his profession, namely: San Ramon, Hollister, Morgan Hill and Perris, Calif. He ended his career before his retirement, as Superintendent of Schools.
