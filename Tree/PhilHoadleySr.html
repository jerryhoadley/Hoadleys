The Hoadley Family

Philip Minor (Phil Sr.)

(James Francis III #51, James Francis, Jr. #43, James Francis #8, Elias #2, Thomas #1), born 2 July, 1918 in Morgan Hill (to be with family doctor who had moved), California. Married 20 September, 1942, to Lois Eva Blake of San Bernardino, California, daughter of Ralph and Ethel Blake and a cousin of Dorothy Johnson. He lived in Perris, Riverside, California, United States for about 8 years. He attended elementary schools in Perris and Perris High School in Perris before graduating from the University of California at Berkley.

Mr. Hoadley moved to Escondido, Calif. and went inot business with his father-in-law (Ralph Blake) in granite business founding Pyramid Granite Co. Inc. of Escondido.

Mr. Hoadley served as the navigator in a of a Bomber crew during World Was II, there is a page about this in the "Stories" section of this site.
