The Hoadley Family

William White ("Pop")

(James Francis III #51, James Francis, Jr. #43, James Francis #8, Elias #2, Thomas #1), born 24 August, 1915 in Crockett, California. Married 23 October, 1938 in Riverside, Calif., to DOROTHY ETHEL JOHNSON, daughter of Emil A. and Nettie May (Young) Johnson, born 23 February, 1915 in Riverside.

Mr. William White Hoadley When William White Hoadley was born on 24 August 1915, in Crockett, California. His father, James Francis Hoadley, was 31 and his mother, Dorothy Burntside White, was 25. He lived in Perris, Riverside, California, United States for about 10 years. He attended elementary schools in Perris and Perris High School in Perris before graduating from Riverside City College in Riverside.

In 1938, Mr. Hoadley obtained employment with the U.S. Customs Service in San Francisco, where he remained until 1947, with time out during World War II to serve with the 303rd Infantry in the U.S. Army, at a Private. In 1948, Mr. Hoadley moved to Escondido, Calif., and obtained employment with his brother Philip Miner Hoadley with the Pyramid Granite Co., Inc., of Escondido, who is the owner of the company. Mr. William Hoadley is the Designer and Surface Plate Technician with the concern. The Hoadleys were affiliated with the First United Methodist Church in Escondido, CA.

William White Hoadley has been most helpful in furnishing information relative to the James Francis III end of the family.
